About KangaCafe

KangaCafe is a neighbourhood café serving handcrafted coffee and seasonal food at 85 Cremorne St, Cremorne VIC 3121.

Our Story

Founded by a local team passionate about great coffee and community, KangaCafe started as a small neighbourhood spot and grew into a place where neighbours gather. The focus is on quality beans, simple fresh food, and friendly service.

Our Values

  • Quality — carefully sourced coffee and fresh ingredients.
  • Sustainability — minimal waste and local suppliers where possible.
  • Community — a welcoming space for everyone.
